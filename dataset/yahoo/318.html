      Severe drought WAS to blame for the collapse of the Mayan civilisation

      The mysterious Mayan civilisation collapsed because of a severe drought, a study has confirmed. Now researchers from the universities of Cambridge and Florida have found strong evidence that a long period of drought had devastating effects on this vast civilisation.
